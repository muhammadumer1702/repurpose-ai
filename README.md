# Repurpose AI

**Turn one webinar, podcast, report, or YouTube video into 10+ platform-ready posts — in seconds.**

Repurpose AI helps consultants, coaches, and content creators instantly transform long-form content into high-performing posts across LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Email, Instagram, and more — all written in **your unique voice**.

### ✨ Key Features
- **Voice Cloning**: Train the AI with 3–5 of your past posts — it learns your tone and style
- **LinkedIn Carousel**: Generates 10 beautiful slides + one-click PDF download
- **Multi-Platform Outputs**:
  - Twitter/X Thread
  - 5-email nurturing sequence
  - Short Video Script + Captions
  - SEO Blog Post
  - Instagram/Threads Captions
- Clean, ready-to-post content
- Persistent voice profiles
- Fast generation powered by OpenAI

### Live URL
[https://muhammadumer1702-repurpose-ai.vercel.app/](https://repurpose--ai.vercel.app/)

### Tech Stack
- Next.js 15 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui
- Supabase (Auth + Database)
- OpenAI (GPT-4o-mini)

### How to Use
1. Sign in
2. Upload or paste your content (YouTube link, audio, PDF, or text)
3. Load or train your voice profile
4. Click **Repurpose This Content**
5. Copy, download PDF, or post directly
